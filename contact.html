FAQs

Questions Before You Reach Out?

What is the fastest way to get a response? +

WhatsApp is our fastest channel — we typically respond within 1 hour during business hours (9 AM – 7 PM IST, Monday to Saturday). Click the floating WhatsApp button on this page to start a chat instantly.

How do I request free product samples? +

We offer free product samples to serious buyers. Simply send us a WhatsApp or email with your name, business name, country, and the product you're interested in. You only need to cover courier shipping costs. Samples are typically dispatched within 5 business days.

Do you work with buyers in my country? +

Yes — we export to 50+ countries globally including the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, Netherlands, UAE, Singapore, Malaysia, South Africa, and many more. Our export team handles all documentation, customs clearance, and logistics for international shipments.

What information should I include in my first inquiry? +

For the most accurate and fastest response, include: your name and company name, your country, the product(s) you're interested in, your approximate order quantity (or if you want samples first), and whether you need standard products or custom/private label. The more detail you provide, the more tailored our response will be.

Can you help with private label and custom branding? +

Absolutely — private label is one of our core services. We handle everything: custom formulation development, label design support, branded packaging (bottles, pouches, jars), all export documentation, and shipping. Contact us to discuss your vision and we'll put together a complete proposal.

What certifications and documentation do you provide? +

We provide a complete documentation package with every shipment: Certificate of Analysis (COA), Certificate of Origin, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, and Phytosanitary Certificate when required. Our facility is ISO certified, GMP certified, Halal certified, USFDA registered, and FSSAI approved.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +

MOQ depends on the product and format. For finished capsule products, MOQ typically starts at 100–500 bottles. For bulk powder, MOQ starts at 1kg. For private label orders, MOQ varies by product but we work with starting quantities to accommodate new brands. Contact us and we'll give you specific MOQs for your products of interest.

How long does shipping and delivery take? +

For ready-stock products, manufacturing and quality testing takes 7–15 business days, plus shipping time. Air freight typically takes 5–10 days internationally. Sea freight takes 15–45 days depending on destination. For custom formulations or private label orders, total lead time is 4–8 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline when you place your order.
