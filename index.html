FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +

We offer flexible MOQ options depending on the product. For most products, MOQ starts from as low as 100–500 units. For bulk wholesale, MOQ may vary. Please contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your specific requirements and we'll provide the best pricing structure.

Do you provide free samples? +

Yes! We offer free product samples for serious buyers. You only need to cover the shipping cost. Samples allow you to verify quality, purity, and effectiveness before placing a bulk order. Use the sample request form above or contact us directly on WhatsApp.

Which certifications do your products have? +

Our manufacturing facility is ISO certified, GMP certified, Halal certified, and USFDA registered. Products are FSSAI approved and third-party lab tested. We provide Certificate of Analysis (COA) and Certificate of Origin with every shipment.

Can you create custom/private label products? +

Absolutely! We specialize in private label and OEM manufacturing. We can create custom formulations, design packaging with your branding, and handle all manufacturing and export. From concept to delivery, we support you every step of the way.

Which countries do you export to? +

We export to 50+ countries worldwide including the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, Germany, France, Netherlands, Singapore, Malaysia, and many more. Our export team handles all documentation, customs, and shipping logistics.

What is the typical lead time for orders? +

For ready-stock products, lead time is 7–15 business days for manufacturing, quality testing, and packaging. For custom formulations or private label orders, lead time is typically 3–6 weeks. Exact timelines depend on order quantity and product type.
